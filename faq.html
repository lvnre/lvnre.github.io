How much herb can fit in the Burnbud?

The storage compartment is located conveniently under the grinder and will hold up to 1½ - 2 grams of legally ground-up herb.


What type of packaging does it come in?

Our packaging comes in an unmarked envelope. Inside the unit is housed in deluxe packaging made from sugar cane, filled with information of the product and its features.


What type of lighter does it hold?

The Burnbud fits a mini Bic lighter. You can purchase the mini Bic lighter at most local gas stations, tobacco shops, or supermarkets. Unfortunately, shipping regulations prohibit the transportation of flammable substances, so we are currently unable to deliver these to your doorstep.


Why is a mini Bic lighter not included with my order?

As a safety precaution, lighters are unable to be stored in our warehouses and shipped via USPS. The flammable butane contained in these lighters prevents us from delivering mini Bic lighters to our customers.
